About Rumi

Rumi, was a 13th-century Persian poet, faqih, Islamic scholar, theologian, and Sufi mystic originally from Greater Khorasan. Rumi's influence transcends national borders and ethnic divisions: Iranians, Tajiks, Turks, Greeks, Pashtuns, other Central Asian Muslims, and the Muslims of South Asia have greatly appreciated his spiritual legacy for the past seven centuries.

His poems have been widely translated into many of the world's languages and transposed into various formats. Rumi has been described as the "most popular poet" and the "best selling poet" in the United States. we dedicated our restaurant after Him,since when I was a kid my dad used to read his poem to us. On that time I decided to have either a library or restaurant named after him, because his poetry was very inspiring for me and I would spend my whole day as a teenager listening and reading his poems.

Rumi Restaurant   serves the most authentic Afghan dishes in a comfortable environment that will make you feel like you are eating in Afghanistan and feel welcomed. Whether you choose one of our spicy meat kabobs or any other option from our menu, there is always something for everyone to love here. Finish off your meal with one of our special desserts that will mark the end to a fine dining experience.
